Ai Animation

How do I create captivating 3D animations with the help of artificial intelligence? By combining artistic vision, technical expertise, and cutting-edge AI tools, I bring characters and scenes to life in innovative ways. Using software like Cascadeur and Rokoko, I streamline the animation process—leveraging Cascadeur’s AI-driven physics to craft realistic movements and Rokoko’s motion capture technology to translate real-world actions into digital performances. Additionally, I harness the power of AI video tools like Runway to generate dynamic sequences, refine keyframes, and experiment with stylized effects. AI accelerates my workflow by automating repetitive tasks, suggesting natural motion paths, and enhancing textures, allowing me to focus on the creative aspects of storytelling. This approach not only showcases my proficiency in 3D animation and modern tools but also highlights my ability to blend art, technology, and AI collaboration, delivering visually stunning and seamless animations. I’m eager to apply this same creativity and innovation to future projects.
